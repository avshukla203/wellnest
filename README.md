WellNest – Project Summary
WellNest is a health and wellness web application currently in its testing and prototype phase. At present, it is a front-end-only project designed to provide users with easy access to essential healthcare information and emergency resources.
Features Implemented
Mother and Child Healthcare Information
Basic guidance on maternal and child health.
Awareness about hygiene, nutrition, and general well-being.
Cleanliness and Hygiene Section
Information promoting healthy habits and personal hygiene practices.
Emergency Numbers in India
Quick access to important emergency helpline numbers for various services.
Home Remedies
Simple home-based remedies and wellness tips for common minor health concerns.
Technology Status
Currently developed as a front-end prototype.
Focused on user interface design and content presentation.
Backend integration, database connectivity, and advanced functionality are planned for future development.
Objective
The goal of WellNest is to create a user-friendly platform that promotes health awareness, provides quick access to emergency information, and supports preventive healthcare through reliable educational content.
Current Development Stage
Status: Testing Phase (Front-End Prototype)
